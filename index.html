    Hello! I am currently pursuing a master degree in Embedded System Design in Germany. My field focuses on System-on-Chip, the technology I find very fascinating and makes me curious about its future. I am focusing mostly on the software side, but I know enough to be coherent about the hardware side. You know what they say, “An expert is someone who has made all the mistakes that can be made in a very narrow field.”


    I received my undergraduate degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Zonguldak Bülent Ecevit University in Zonguldak, Türkiye. During my studies, I had the chance to do the Erasmus program at the University of Vigo for one semester. I specialized in Telecommunication during my undergraduate degree. In my final year, I met with Embedded Systems, a field that made me very excited, and I decided to move forward in this area.


    In 2025, I began my M.Sc in Embedded System Design at Bremerhaven University of Applied , Sciences. Our program relies on System-on-Chip technology such as ZYNQ devices, model-based software development, real-time software development, and safety-critical system engineering. Developing algorithms and software excites me, and the combination of HW/SW is something I find very interesting; this is the core reason I chose to pursue a master’s degree in this area. I have completed my Embedded System Project, which is one of the modules, in the Projects section. I have hands-on experience with platforms such as Zynq-7 devices and STM32Fx series. I mostly work on ARM architecture. I have knowledge of various RTOSes such as FreeRTOS, ThreadX, and µC/OS-II. Additionally, in my latest , project I worked on Embedded Linux. I also follow the latest trends in the embedded world, such as DevOps and test-driven development.


    So far it has been technical, now I want to talk about the fun part of me :) I really enjoy creating content. It is mainly about Embedded Systems, and you can find more in the YouTube section. I love sports in general, but Calisthenics is close to my heart. I do calisthenics very often. I am a young man who tries to develop himself, so I read as much as possible.
